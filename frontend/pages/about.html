We believe that landing your dream job shouldn't be a game of chance. AutoCV uses advanced AI to level the playing field for every candidate.

The Problem

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) reject over 75% of qualified resumes before a human ever sees them. This "black hole" of recruitment prevents talented people from making their mark.

Our Solution

AutoCV provides the same intelligence used by recruiters to candidates. We analyze your resume against real-world ATS benchmarks and provide actionable, instant optimizations.
