The Story Behind AutoCV

We built AutoCV to level the playing field between candidates and Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Our Mission

Today, over 95% of Fortune 500 companies use ATS to screen resumes. Unfortunately, great candidates often get filtered out simply because their resume isn't formatted correctly or is missing specific keywords that the system is looking for.

AutoCV uses advanced parsing logic and AI to help job seekers see exactly what these systems see, and more importantly, how to fix it before hitting "Apply".

Dual-Sided Efficiency

For Students

Land more interviews by ensuring your resume is a perfect match for the roles you want. No more guessing what the recruiter is looking for.

For Recruiters

Screen large volumes of applicants instantly. Sort by relevance and focus your energy on the candidates who actually meet the requirements.
