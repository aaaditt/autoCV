Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my Pro subscription at any time? expand_more
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my resume after 7 days on the Single plan? expand_more
Your resume remains stored in your account, but you will no longer be able to export it or use AI optimization features. You can reactivate access by purchasing another Single credit or upgrading to Pro.
Does AutoCV work with ATS systems? expand_more
Absolutely. All our templates are rigorously tested against popular Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to ensure your resume is correctly parsed and ranked.
