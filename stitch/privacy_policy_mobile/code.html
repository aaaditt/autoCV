Privacy Policy

We take your career data seriously. This document outlines how ResumeAI protects your personal information and resume history.

Last Updated

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, upload a resume, or communicate with our AI optimization tools. This includes:

  • check_circle Contact details (name, email address).
  • check_circle Professional history and educational background.
  • check_circle Skills, certifications, and project descriptions.

2. AI Processing

Our proprietary AI models process your resume text to provide optimization suggestions. We do not sell your resume data to third-party recruiters without your explicit consent.

Data Retention

Resumes are stored securely for as long as your account is active. You can request permanent deletion at any time.

3. Your Rights

Data Portability

You have the right to export your data in a machine-readable format at any time from your account settings.

Right to Erasure

Closing your account triggers a 30-day "soft-delete" period, after which all data is scrubbed from our servers.
